List Tags

Unordered Lists

Unordered lists are where the order doesn't matter and each list item in an unordered list begins with a bullet point.

  • Cheese
  • Coffee
  • Bread

Ordered Lists

Orderd lists are where the order matter and each list item in an ordered list begins with a number instead of a bullet point.

  1. Do laundry
  2. Clean the room
  3. Read your favorite book
